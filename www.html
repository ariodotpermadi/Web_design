Entrepreneurship & Innovation Summit 2021

To realize the positive benefit of disrupsion and change in our world, together with Smart Change Initiative, we are proud to host Entrepreneurship & Innovation Summit 2021. Our strategic theme this year is "Resilient Entrepreneurship Ecosystem : An engine for job creation and inclusive growth in Indonesia", focusing on providing connections and insight from some of the initiatives conducted by Smart Change.

Entrepreneurship & Innovation Summit 2021 is part of Smart Change initiative, a unique collaboration of the local authorities Jakarta, Berlin, Bangkok empowering Smart City and Urban Innovation Ecosystems. The Summit offers a premiere opportunity to build connections and to pursue new solution within the Entrepreneurship Ecosystem.
